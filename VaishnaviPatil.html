About Kolhapur

Kolhapur is a historic city in the Indian state of Maharashtra, known for its rich culture, ancient temples, and delicious cuisine.

Key Attractions

        Shree Mahalaxmi Temple - One of the prominent temples in India.
        Rankala Lake - A scenic spot for relaxation and boating.
        Panhala Fort - A historic fort offering picturesque views.

Famous Food

You must try the famous Kolhapuri Misal and Kolhapuri Bhel Kolhapuri Tambada Rassa for a spicy delight.

Geography

The city is located at a latitude of 16.7° N and longitude of 74.2° E. It lies at an altitude of 569 meters above sea level.

Education and Culture

Kolhapur is also a hub for education and has several reputed colleges and universities. The cultural heritage is reflected through its traditional Lavani dance and local festivals.
